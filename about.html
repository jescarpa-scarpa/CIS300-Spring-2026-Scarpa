About Accurate Electrical and Mechanical Contractors, Inc.

At Accurate Electrical and Mechanical Contractors, Inc., our work has always been built on experience, consistency, and doing things the right way. We take pride in delivering reliable service and building long-term relationships with our customers through quality work and dependable results.

Company Background

Our company has been owned and operated by Joseph Scarpa for over 30 years. Throughout that time, we have grown through steady work, repeat customers, and a strong reputation for reliability. Being a family-run business, we focus on maintaining a level of service that keeps customers comfortable coming back and referring others.

Leadership

Our leadership is hands-on and directly involved in both field operations and day-to-day business decisions. From scheduling and pricing to project oversight, everything is managed with a focus on accountability and experience.

Services Provided

Our team takes on a wide range of electrical and mechanical work, from day-to-day service calls to larger construction projects. No matter the job, the focus stays the same: get the work done efficiently, safely, and to a high standard.

What Sets Us Apart

What sets us apart is our commitment to being organized, dependable, and detail-oriented in every project. A large portion of our work comes from referrals, which reflects the trust our customers place in us.
